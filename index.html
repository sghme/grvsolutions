Our Story

GlobalReach Virtual Solutions is a subsidiary of CMJ Express Distribution, a growing business group based in Ormoc City, Leyte, Philippines.

CMJ Express Distribution was established in October 2020 and is located along Nadongholan St., Brgy. North, Ormoc City. Since its founding, the company has focused on the distribution of rice and essential household goods to retail stores across the Leyte area.

Another subsidiary under the CMJ group is Afforda-Meds Inc., which operates in the pharmaceutical retail sector. Afforda-Meds franchises Generika Drugstore, a well-known Philippine drugstore brand that offers affordable and high-quality generic medicines. The company currently manages Generika branches in Ormoc City, Kananga, and Baybay City.

Through continuous expansion and the support of modern business systems, CMJ Express Distribution and its subsidiaries remain committed to delivering reliable products and services to the communities they serve.
